# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for
everyone. Participants are expected to be respectful and considerate in all project spaces —
issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other interactions.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, personal attacks, and other conduct
that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to ospo@kiteworks.com. All reports will be
reviewed and handled with discretion.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
